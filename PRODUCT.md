# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Marketers and performance advertisers who run campaigns on Meta (Facebook/Instagram). They sit at a desk with the platform open alongside Meta Business Suite, working in a task-driven workflow: create campaign, monitor spend, read the AI's analysis, adjust. They are comfortable with advertising vocabulary (CTR, ROAS, CPM, ad sets, creatives) and impatient with anything that slows them down.

Secondary persona: agency account managers who oversee multiple client accounts and need a quick read on performance before a client call.

## Product Purpose

AdMind AI lets advertisers create, manage, and analyze Meta Ads campaigns through a natural-language AI agent backed by a full analytics dashboard. The agent handles campaign briefs and creation via the Meta API; the dashboard surfaces performance metrics, audience data, and page analytics. Success is an advertiser who can brief and launch a campaign in under 2 minutes and read their week's performance without leaving the app.

## Brand Personality

Precise, Analytical, Confident. The interface knows what it's doing and doesn't hedge. Answers are direct, numbers are prominent, and interactions complete without ceremony. Intelligence shows in what is omitted, not added.

## Anti-references

- Warm neutral SaaS backgrounds (Notion, Coda, Craft): cream, sand, paper, or linen backgrounds signal "productivity tool" not "performance platform."
- Visual theater for its own sake (early Vercel aesthetic, Linear marketing): heavy gradient heroes, dramatic typography reveals, motion as brand expression. The user is mid-task; they didn't come to watch.

## Design Principles

1. **Data at the surface.** Numbers, trends, and status should be readable at a glance without hovering, clicking, or reading prose summaries.
2. **The tool disappears into the task.** Every interaction should be faster than the thought that triggered it. Friction is a feature failure.
3. **Confidence without decoration.** State and hierarchy through weight, spacing, and contrast, not color quantity. The dark palette is a workspace, not a stage.
4. **Consistency earns trust.** Same button shape, same form controls, same icon weight across every screen. Inconsistency signals unreliability to users who stake budget on the tool.
5. **The AI is a collaborator, not a mascot.** The AI agent speaks in the user's language (French/English), presents structured briefs for review before acting, and surfaces insights that change decisions, not just summaries of what the user already sees.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG 2.1 AA minimum. Bilingual interface (French and English); copy should be natural in both, not translated literally. Ensure all interactive states (hover, focus, active, disabled, error) are distinguishable without color alone.
